The Best Movies According to Sam

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Last Samurai

I really love the scenery in ancient Japan, the rich culture and humble people. Follow the way of Samurai with pride and discipline which is hard to find in the world today.

Kung Fu Panda

This movie has lot of cultural influence of ancient China. Quite humorous way to teach you about self acceptance and respect.

The Matrix

Action packed drama between humans and machines. Has a deep meaning within the war that tries reflects our reality quite accurately. Great to watch with children especially boys.
